How to Find Sticky Numbers
What are sticky numbers?

Sticky numbers refers to the positions on the Master Lock® dial where, when the shackle is pulled taught, the dial "sticks" in place between two numbers.

The middle of these two numbers is the sticky number. For instance, if you pull the shackle taught, turn the dial counterclockwise and the dial "sticks" on 3 and 5, the sticky number is 4. If it were 4 and 5, the number would be 4.5.

Steps to get sticky numbers
  1. Turn the dial clockwise (numbers descending) about three times. This clears the tumbler of any previous turns.
  2. On the last turn to clear the tumbler, stop at number 0.
  3. Apply firm and continuous upward force on the shackle.
  4. Begin to turn the dial counterclockwise until it sticks and goes no further.
  5. You should be able to wiggle the dial between that first stuck number and another. Take note, or type into the app, the number that falls between the two stuck numbers. Remember: if the dial sticks between two adjacent numbers (ex: 21 and 22), the sticky number is 21.5.
  6. Continue this all the way around the dial, up to number 39.
  7. You should have 12 sticky numbers. Return to the previous page and input those numbers in order.
